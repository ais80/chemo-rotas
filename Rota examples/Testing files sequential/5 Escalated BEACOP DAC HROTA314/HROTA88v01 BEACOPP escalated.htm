Tests

Renal

Renal

GFR 15-50ml/min – Give 75% of dose

Hepatic

ALT >60-180U/L – Give 50% of dose

ALT > 180U/L – Clinical decision

Renal

No adjustment

Bilirubin > 51µmmol/L and ALT > 180U/L – Omit

Bilirubin < 26µmmol/L and ALT > 180U/L – Omit

Precautions

Bleomycin: If shortness of breath, CXR needed before further bleomycin can be given. Discontinue if any evidence of fibrosis/infiltration or transfer factor <50%.

Prednisolone: Dose on days 1-3 is 20mg/m2 due to the interaction with Akynzeo. History of peptic ulcer – give lansoprazole 30mg daily. Risk of hyperglycaemia if diabetic.

Vincristine: NCI grade II – 33% reduction; NCI grade II motor/grade II sensory – 50% reduction; NCI grade III motor or grade IV sensory – omit.

Allopurinol: Reduce dose of for patients with renal impairment.
